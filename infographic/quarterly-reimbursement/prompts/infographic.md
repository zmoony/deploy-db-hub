# Infographic Prompt: 季度报销去哪儿了？

A humorous kawaii-style cartoon illustration about delayed quarterly reimbursement, portrait 9:16 aspect ratio.

**Main Scene**: 
A cute, big-eyed office worker character sitting at a desk with an empty wallet open in front of them. The wallet has cobwebs and a tiny crying face. Above the worker, a thought bubble shows a calendar with all four seasons (spring flowers, summer sun, autumn leaves, winter snow) already passed, with text "季度都过完了..." (The quarter is over...).

- A large arrow on the calendar saying "报销还在路上..." (Reimbursement is still on the way...)
